            ## FAQs
            ### Attractions FAQs:

*Question:* What attractions are available at Sky Zone?
*Answer:* Sky Joust, Sky Slam, Ultimate Dodgeball, Drop Zone, Free style Jump, and Toddler Zone..

*Question:* Is there a Rock-Climbing Wall?
*Answer:* The Rock-Climbing Wall attraction is no longer available in Sky Zone Elk Grove..

### Food & Payment FAQs:

*Question:* What outside food and drinks can we bring?
*Answer:* There will be a $50 fee to bring outside food and drinks All outside food must be pre-packaged or store-bought. Customers with passes can purchase food in Fuel zone and are not allowed to bring outside food or drinks. Home-cooked food is not allowed. Only store-bought and prepackaged food is permitted..

*Question:* Do you accept cash or card payments?
*Answer:* Yes, we accept cash, credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay..

*Question:* Can we bring food/drink for a walk-in with jump pass?
*Answer:* We do not allow outside food or drinks at our facility for a walk-in..

### General FAQs:

*Question:* Do you offer free service water?
*Answer:* Yes! We provide free pitchers of water..

*Question:* Do you have a freezer?
*Answer:* A refrigerator is available to store cakes or ice cream cakes for parties..

*Question:* Do you have Wi-Fi?
*Answer:* Yes! Free Wi-Fi is available..

*Question:* Do you provide candles?
*Answer:* We do not provide candles.

*Question:* Do you offer military or first responder discounts?
*Answer:* Yes! 10% discount for military personnel and first responders. Discounts apply to tickets and parties. Must be redeemed at the front desk with valid ID..

*Question:* Do you have promotions?
*Answer:* 15% off weekday parties (Mon-Thurs): Use code 10-B-DAY-WEEK. Thursday Family Fun Pack (in-app only): 4 standard passes, 1 large cheese pizza, and a fountain pitcher for $75..

*Question:* Can we use membership at any SkyZone?
*Answer:* No, memberships are location specfic. You can only use membership at the park where you sign up..

*Question:* Do you offer gift cards?
*Answer:* Yes, digital gift cards are available for purchase online. Physical gift cards are not available..

*Question:* Can we use tickets at any SkyZone?
*Answer:* No, tickets are site specifc. You can only use the tickets at the park from where you purchase those tickets..

*Question:* Do Minors have to be supervised by adults during open Jump times?
*Answer:* No, if minors are fourteen and above and their waivers have been signed then they can be left unattended at Sky Zone for Open Jump times. However, Minors that are seventeen or under have to be supervised by a parent or a legal guardian during Glow events..

*Question:* How old must a child be for Little Leapers?

*Question:* How much does a Glow T-Shirt cost?

*Answer:* Five dollars per pair..

*Question:* What are VIP Passes?

*Question:* Can I reuse my Sky Zone Socks?
*Answer:* Yes, if they are in good condition and properly washed..

### Group Booking FAQs:

*Question:* When can i book group events?
*Answer:* Group events can be booked Monday-Thursday at any time and Fridays from 11am-3pm.

### Safety & Rules FAQs:

*Question:* Do you allow jumpers with hard cast or injuries?
*Answer:* No, for safety reasons, injured individuals or those with hard cast are not allowed to participate..

*Question:* Do you allow backflips?
*Answer:* No. Running, double bouncing, double flips, gainers, and diving are strictly prohibited..

## Policies
*All Day Pass Check-In Check-Out Policy*:
The All-Day Pass provides unlimited jump time; however, you must stay at the location to use it continuously. If you check out, you will not be allowed to re-enter using the same pass..

*Birthday Party Booking Policy*:
A minimum deposit of 50% is required to complete your booking. Deposits for last minute parties (within 7 days) are non-refundable..

*Birthday Party Cancellation Policy*:
If you cancel your party more than 7 days in advance, you will get a refund of any deposit you have put down. Cancelling 7 days or less before the day of the party means you forfeit any deposits put down on the event..

*Birthday Party Decoration Policy*:
PARTY ROOM DECORATIONS You are permitted to bring your own decorations for the party room. However, please note: pinatas, glitter, silly string, and confetti are not permitted. Balloons with weights are allowed, as well as tablecloths. Your party host will help you set up decorations 15 minutes before your room time. Do not bring items that tape or stick to the walls..

*Birthday Party Rescheduling Policy*:
Parties get 1 free reschedule as long as they notify us more than 7 days in advance. typically if the Guest of Honour is sick we will go ahead and proceed with the reschedule. we charge $200 reschedule fee when guest is just wanting to move the date only due to other reason (e.g guest decided last minute that they couldn't make it. etc.).

*Epic Party Package And Basic Party Package
Maximum Guests And Jumpers Policy*:
The Epic party package and Basic party package allow up to 50 total guests, with a maximum of 30 jumpers included..

*Glow Package Policy*:
NO DROP OFFS for minors 17 yrs and below, PARENTS MUST BE PRESENT TO ENTER AND STAY THE FULL 2 HOURS OF GLOW..

*Glow Session Glow T-Shirt Policy*:
Glow shirts not required — guests can wear neon pink, green, or white (but not orange)..

*Jumping Policy*:
All jumpers in your party must have a valid Sky Zone Las Vegas waiver, wristband, Sky Socks.

*Mega Vip Package Maximum Jumpers Policy*:
The Mega VIP package allows up to 45 jumpers..

*Membership Cancellation Policy*:
MEMBERSHIP CANCELLATION CAN BE DONE OVER THE CALL. THEY CAN CANCEL THE MEMBERSHIP WITHIN 3 BUSINESS DAYS OF BILLING DATE. For guests who request an email confirmation of their membership cancellation, please note that they will automatically receive an email on the final day of their billing cycle. Instead of stating that a charge has been processed, this email will confirm that their membership has been cancelled..
