## FAQs
### Attractions FAQs:

*Question:* What attractions are available at Sky Zone?
*Answer:* Sky Joust, Sky Slam, Ultimate Dodgeball, Drop Zone, Free style Jump, and Toddler Zone..

*Question:* Is there a Rock-Climbing Wall?
*Answer:* The Rock-Climbing Wall attraction is no longer available in Sky Zone Elk Grove..

### Food & Payment FAQs:

*Question:* What outside food and drinks can we bring?
*Answer:* There will be a $50 fee to bring outside food and drinks All outside food must be pre-packaged or store-bought. Customers with passes can purchase food in Fuel zone and are not allowed to bring outside food or drinks. Home-cooked food is not allowed. Only store-bought and prepackaged food is permitted..

*Question:* Do you accept cash or card payments?
*Answer:* Yes, we accept cash, credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay..

*Question:* Can we bring food/drink for a walk-in with jump pass?
*Answer:* We do not allow outside food or drinks at our facility for a walk-in..

### General FAQs:

*Question:* Do you offer free service water?
*Answer:* Yes! We provide free pitchers of water..

*Question:* Do you have a freezer?
*Answer:* A refrigerator is available to store cakes or ice cream cakes for parties..

*Question:* Do you have Wi-Fi?
*Answer:* Yes! Free Wi-Fi is available..

*Question:* Do you provide candles?
*Answer:* We do not provide candles.

*Question:* Do you offer military or first responder discounts?
*Answer:* Yes! 10% discount for military personnel and first responders. Discounts apply to tickets and parties. Must be redeemed at the front desk with valid ID..

The All-Day Pass provides unlimited jump time; however, you must stay at the location to use it continuously. If you check out, you will not be allowed to re-enter using the same pass..

If you cancel your party more than 7 days in advance, you will get a refund of any deposit you have put down. Cancelling 7 days or less before the day of the party means you forfeit any deposits put down on the event..
